### Step 4 — On-Page SEO Fixes (Day 5–7)
**Objective:** make every page understandable to Google.

#### A) Title Tags & Meta Descriptions
**Homepage Title (example)**
- *IT Solutions Company in Casablanca | Custom Software, Cloud & AI – Digitalia Solutions*

**Homepage Meta Description (example)**
- *Digitalia helps businesses grow with custom software, AI solutions, and secure cloud services in Morocco. Contact us for a free consultation.*

**Rules**
- Title: 50–60 characters (approx)
- Meta: 140–160 characters (approx)
- Include primary keyword + brand
- Use a benefit + CTA

#### B) Heading Structure Rules
- Only **one H1 per page**
- H2 sections should cover:
  - Services
  - Process
  - Case studies / Proof
  - FAQs

#### C) Image SEO
- Rename files to descriptive keywords:
  - `cloud-migration-casablanca.jpg`
- Add ALT text:
  - “Cloud migration services in Casablanca for business applications”

**Deliverables**
- SEO metadata sheet (page → title → meta)
- Updated heading outline for each page

---

### Step 5 — Create Dedicated Service Pages (Week 2)
**Objective:** rank for high-intent keywords and increase leads.

Create pages like:
1. `/custom-software-development`
2. `/cloud-solutions-migration`
3. `/data-analytics-business-intelligence`
4. `/ai-machine-learning-solutions`
5. `/digital-transformation-it-consulting`

**Service Page Template**
1. H1: Service keyword + location (if relevant)
2. What the service is (clear, simple)
3. Problems you solve (bullets)
4. Deliverables (bullets)
5. Process (3–5 steps)
6. Tools/tech stack (only what you actually use)
7. Mini case study / example (even if anonymized)
8. FAQs (5–8)
9. CTA: consultation

**Deliverables**
- 5 service page copy drafts
- Internal links from homepage + services hub

---

### Step 6 — Build a Blog Plan (Week 2–4)
**Objective:** capture informational searches and build authority.

**Publishing plan**
- 2 articles/month to start (then scale)

**Topic ideas (examples)**
- Cloud Migration: common mistakes & best practices
- AI for SMEs: practical use cases
- Business Intelligence dashboards: what to track
- Cybersecurity basics for growing companies
- Choosing between AWS/Azure/GCP (based on your real offering)

**Rules**
- Each blog targets one keyword
- Add internal links to relevant services
- Add a strong CTA at the end (“Talk to an expert”)

**Deliverables**
- 3-month editorial calendar
- 6 blog outlines

---

### Step 7 — Technical SEO Improvements (Week 2–3)
**Objective:** ensure the site is fast, crawlable, and healthy.

**Checklist**
- Confirm SSL works everywhere (no mixed content)
- Add/verify:
  - sitemap.xml
  - robots.txt
- Fix:
  - broken links (404s)
  - duplicate titles/meta
- Speed:
  - compress images
  - lazy-load images
  - minimize scripts
- Add canonical tags where needed

**Deliverables**
- Technical SEO issue list + fixes completed

---

### Step 8 — Schema + Rich Results (Week 3)
**Objective:** improve visibility in Google with structured data.

**Recommended schema**
- Organization schema
- LocalBusiness schema (if applicable)
- FAQ schema (on pages with FAQs)
- Service schema (if supported in your setup)

**Deliverables**
- Implemented schema + validation results

---

### Step 9 — Local SEO Boost (Optional but Strong)
**Objective:** rank better in Morocco/Casablanca searches.

**Checklist**
- Add consistent NAP (Name/Address/Phone) in footer + Contact
- Create/optimize Google Business Profile (if you serve locally)
- Add location signals in copy naturally:
  - Casablanca, Morocco, region served

**Deliverables**
- Local SEO checklist completed

---

### Step 10 — Tracking & Iteration (Ongoing)
**Objective:** measure impact and keep improving.

**Set up**
- Google Analytics (GA4)
- Google Search Console
- Conversion tracking:
  - form submits
  - call clicks
  - WhatsApp clicks (if used)

**Monthly review**
- Top pages by impressions
- Queries gaining traction
- Pages with high impressions but low CTR → improve titles/meta
- Pages with traffic but low conversions → improve CTAs and layout
